# 🪙 ERGO Tokenomics & Incentives

The ERGO token lies at the heart of the Ergo ecosystem's utility and governance system.

## Token Utility

- **Governance Votes**: Vote-locked ERGO grants proposal and voting power.
- **Ecosystem Rewards**: Distributed to lenders, borrowers, and backstop pool depositors.
- **Liquidation Discounts**: Holding ERGO unlocks higher discounts in Dutch auctions.
