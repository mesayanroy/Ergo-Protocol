# 🗳️ Governance & Proposal Workflows

Ergo Protocol is governed by a decentralized autonomous workflow driven by ERGO token holders.

## Proposal Rules

- **Fee**: A proposal fee of `50 ERGO` is paid to submit any proposal.
- **Voting Period**: Voting remains active for exactly 5 days.
- **Quorum**: Requires at least 4% of the total circulating ERGO supply to vote.
- **Timelock**: Approved changes are subject to a 48-hour timelock execution delay.
